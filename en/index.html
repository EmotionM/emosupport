About EMo

EMo is a simple, easy-to-use mood diary that helps you record daily emotions, build healthy habits, and reflect on mood trends.

Your data stays on your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, data is encrypted and stored in your private iCloud account.

FAQs

1. How do I get started?

  • On the home screen, tap “+” to record a mood.
  • Create daily habit reminders to build positive routines.
  • Open “Footprints” to view your weekly summaries.

2. Is my data uploaded?

  • By default, all data is stored locally on your device.
  • If iCloud sync is enabled, data is encrypted and synced to your private iCloud (Apple ID).
  • We cannot access, view, or analyze your personal data.

3. Can I delete all data?

  • Yes. You can delete all data at any time in the app settings.
  • You can also disable iCloud sync whenever you want.

4. Is my data shared or used for ads?

  • No data is shared with third parties.
  • Data is not used for advertising or behavioral analysis.
  • Smart suggestions work entirely on your device.
