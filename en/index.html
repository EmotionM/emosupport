About EMo

EMo is a simple, easy-to-use mood diary that helps you record daily emotions, build healthy habits, and reflect on mood trends.

Your data stays on your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, data is encrypted and stored in your private iCloud account.

FAQs

1. How do I get started?

  • On the home screen, tap “+” to record a mood.
  • Create daily habit reminders to build positive routines.
  • Open “Footprints” to view your weekly summaries.

2. Is my data uploaded?

  • By default, all data is stored locally on your device.
  • If iCloud sync is enabled, data is encrypted and synced to your private iCloud (Apple ID).
  • We cannot access, view, or analyze your personal data.

3. How do I enable iCloud sync?

  • On iPhone, go to Settings → your Apple ID → iCloud → iCloud Drive → enable EMo.
  • Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID on all your devices.

4. Can I export my data?

  • Currently, EMo focuses on on-device privacy. Export features will be considered in future updates.

5. Subscription & trial

  • EMo offers optional subscription to unlock premium features.
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription in App Store → Account → Subscriptions.

6. I found a bug / want to suggest a feature

  • Please send us details via email: contactemotionm@gmail.com.
  • Include device model, iOS version, EMo version, and steps to reproduce if possible.
